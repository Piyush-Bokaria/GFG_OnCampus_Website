Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter?

You can join by attending our recruitment events or by contacting us through our social media channels. We welcome all students who are passionate about coding and technology.

Are the events free to attend?

Yes, most of our events are completely free for students. Some special workshops or hackathons might have a nominal registration fee to cover materials and refreshments.

Do I need prior programming experience to participate?

Not at all! We have events and workshops designed for beginners as well as advanced programmers. Our community is very welcoming to students at all skill levels.

How often do you conduct events?

We typically conduct 2-3 events per month, including workshops, tech talks, coding competitions, and hackathons. Follow our social media for regular updates.

Can I get certificates for attending events?

Yes, we provide certificates of participation for most events and certificates of achievement for competitions and hackathons. These can be valuable for your resume.

How can I stay updated about upcoming events?

Follow us on our social media channels, join our WhatsApp group, or check our website regularly. We also send email notifications to registered members.
